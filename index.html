Band Formation

Fifth Harmony, was an all girls band that was formed in Miami, U.S.A. Interestungly, the band was formed out of chance after the girls auditoned solo in the 2012 X-factors. After Simon Cowell saw the individual talents of the girls, he asked them to form a band instead since they had all failed the audition stages. The band was formed with the girls opting for LYAS "lOVE YOU LIKE A SISTER" as a name. However, Bruno Mar's ssters had the name first and started a rumoured feud between them. Simon suggested getting a name from a fan page that gave them the Fifth Harmony.

Years Active

The band was officially formed during the season two of X-Factor in 2012. They became third place in December 20, 2012. After X-factor, the group was taken by Simon's label, Syco Music. The girl's releaed their first song, Miss Movin' on. The single hit song became number 76 in the Billboard Hot 100. At the helm of 2016, Cammila left the group after working on their second studio alnum "Work from Home". The remaining members took a break till the 24th of JUly, 2017 where they performed for the first time since Camilla's exit in December 2016.

Current Status of the band

The band are still together performing across the U.S and the world. Their songs continue hitting the billboard charts as one of the best pop singles and albums from an all girls' band.
